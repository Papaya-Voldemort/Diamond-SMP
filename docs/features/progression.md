# Progression

Progression is the defining design constraint of the plugin.

## Philosophy

Diamond SMP is not trying to balance vanilla endgame. It removes vanilla access to the top tier and replaces it with event-gated advancement.

## Blocked Vanilla Paths

- netherite upgrading
- ancient debris access from fresh Nether terrain
- high-end loot table shortcuts
- Protection IV and Sharpness V progression through normal enchanting or anvil paths
- Strength II brewing and usage

## Replacement Path

1. Run an event.
2. Win the event.
3. Receive a reward villager.
4. Buy from limited stock.
5. Retire that stock globally.

This makes progression social, competitive, and seasonal instead of purely grind-based.

## Secondary Progression Accelerators

- boosted diamond economy
- easy golden apples
- easy cobweb crafting
- kit-based PvP practice without risking SMP inventories
