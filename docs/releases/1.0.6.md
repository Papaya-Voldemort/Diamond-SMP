# Diamond SMP 1.0.6

Diamond SMP `1.0.6` is an economy and villager balance patch focused on making diamonds valuable again without turning normal progression into a grind.

## Highlights

- Tones down new-chunk diamond inflation while boosting iron, gold, and coal through configurable ore rewrite settings
- Removes the managed reward villager despawn timer and lets those villagers be relocated through tagged egg drops
- Adds a prestige enchanted golden apple craft that costs eight full stacks of diamond blocks around one golden apple
- Adds expensive diamond sink books: `Prospector`, `Bulwark`, and `Momentum`
- Adds rare extra diamond-heavy trades to normal master villagers
- Raises god-item costs so endgame purchases feel aspirational again
